## What is DAWN Protocol?

**DAWN Protocol** is a decentralized wireless network enabling users to **buy and sell Internet capacity** within their local areas. It empowers property owners and residents to operate as local Internet service providers (ISPs), bypassing traditional ISP intermediaries.  

The protocol combines **hardware and software solutions**, fostering a peer-to-peer network for Internet connectivity while expanding decentralized Internet access.
