## About Pixel Protocol

Pixel Protocol is a new NFT platform that aims to revolutionize digital art creation and trading. The protocol introduces innovative features for artists and collectors.

## Participation Guide

1. **Create an Account**
   - Visit Pixel Protocol website
   - Connect your Web3 wallet
   - Complete profile verification

2. **Mint Your First NFT**
   - Choose from available collections
   - Pay gas fees in ETH
   - Confirm transaction

3. **Trading Requirements**
   - List or buy at least one NFT
   - Complete minimum one trade
   - Hold tokens until snapshot

## Rewards Structure

- Basic participation: $200
- Active traders: Up to $1000
- Governance participants: Additional $1000
