## What is ZetaChain?

ZetaChain is a blockchain designed for seamless cross-chain transactions and DeFi operations. It enables users to interact with multiple chains without switching networks.

1. **Liquidity Provision**

   - Maintain position for minimum 14 days
   - Minimum value: $1000

2. **Cross-Chain Activities**
   - Use Zeta Bridge
   - Execute cross-chain swaps
   - Complete at least 5 transactions

3. **Governance Participation**
   - Vote on proposals
   - Join community discussions
   - Submit improvement suggestions
