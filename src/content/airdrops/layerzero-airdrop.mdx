## What is LayerZero?

LayerZero is an omnichain interoperability protocol that enables direct communication between different blockchains. It provides a powerful infrastructure for cross-chain applications and messaging.

## How to Participate

1. **Connect Your Wallet**
   - Install MetaMask or any Web3 wallet
   - Ensure you have sufficient funds on supported chains

2. **Bridge Assets**
   - Visit LayerZero's official bridge
   - Connect your wallet
   - Select source and destination chains
   - Choose the amount to bridge
   - Complete at least 5 transactions

3. **Use Multiple Chains**
   - Bridge to at least 3 different chains
   - Maintain activity on each chain
   - Total value bridged should exceed $500

## Eligibility Criteria

- Must complete transactions before March 30, 2024
- Minimum total value bridged: $500
- At least 5 successful bridge transactions
- Activity spread across 3 or more chains

## Tips for Success

- Start with smaller amounts to test the process
- Keep track of your transactions
- Diversify across different chains
- Stay updated with official announcements
